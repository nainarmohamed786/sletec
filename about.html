About US

SLETEC is powered by senior engineers, designers, analysts, and manufacturing specialists with backgrounds in major Aramco, SABIC and international EPC projects. Our holistic approach integrates multi‑disciplinary engineering, simulation, manufacturability, compliance, safety engineering, and full documentation support.

Mission

Deliver engineering solutions ensuring quality, safety, performance and compliance.

Vision

Become a leading global engineering consultancy bridging KSA industry needs and India design excellence.

Core Strength

Senior technical leadership

Advanced engineering tools

We Deliver

  • Full multi‑disciplinary engineering under one roof (Mechanical, Piping, Civil, Structural, Electrical, Automation, FEA, CFD).
  • Dual‑country execution model (KSA + India) for speed, accuracy and cost-efficiency.
  • Aramco, SABIC, SATORP, Ma’aden documentation expertise ensuring smooth approvals.
  • Strong engineering team with mega‑project exposure.
  • Advanced engineering tools: Creo, Inventor, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, ANSYS, STAAD, CFD.
  • Manufacturing & machining excellence (Jigs, Fixtures, CNC, GD&T, DFM/DFA).
